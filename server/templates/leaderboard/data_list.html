Challenge Data Sets

Below are links to download the currently available data. The main links are to files hosted in London, and the US mirror are hosted in Pasadena, CA. 16 of the 20 branches are available in both places.

Please report any problems downloading the datasets to challenge@great3challenge.info or post an issue at github.
