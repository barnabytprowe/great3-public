As of October 17, the ~6 month time period for the GREAT3 challenge has begun, with submissions due by April 30, 2014. All 20 challenge branches are available for download.

We plan soon to apply an overall metric recalculation to all methods. Therefore, do not be perturbed if you see a small change in your metric score in any given branch leaderboard over the coming days. For constant shear, this will be an effective (nonlinear) renomalization so that target-meeting methods score Q~1000. All changes will be made fairly across all methods and will not alter relative rankings between methods. For variable shear, the change is slightly more complex - an announcement will go out on the participants list to explain the need for and nature of this change.
